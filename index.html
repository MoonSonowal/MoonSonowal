Role

Year

The concept of this app was to provide target end users with fishing location, booking facilities, types of fish a particular spot is famous for, guides, share rating, etc. It was developed in collaboration with a client to produce a demo app to for concept pitching for investors.




The approach was to categories the audicence into two different categories, namely fresh water and salt water, which would further categorize the availability of data dynamically.
