Call Disposition

Most of the time sales managers have concerns around their representatives field activity. Are they making visits ? Are FS visits happening at the quality I would expect? From FS point of view it was required for them to collect and verify documents. Doing that manually was time consuming and very difficult to track. The Field sales application with call disposition feature was an answer to it.
With this feature a field executive can login to the app, check the meeting scheduled for the day and based on the location plan their meetings. To keep a track of the representative activity they would dispose a card assigned to them by selecting a reason of disposition be it rescheduling, customer unavailability or completion of assigned task.

Billing

The aim was to design and develop a billing system that is capable and reliable in the whole transaction flow such as tracking, retrieving and storing data in an appropriate way.
1. Understanding plans, usage of dashboard by partners.
2. Research included studying the different subscription plans that existed.
3. Flexibility was provided to change plans in future as and when required.
4. Different options to view and download previous months bills.
5. Breakdown of invoice with proper tax details with online payment options.
6. Different card options were provided, with flexibility to add or remove a card for payment.
